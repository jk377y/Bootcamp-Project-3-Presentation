April 17th, 2023

In the beginning...

ChatGPT launched November 30th, 2022

It was wildly accepted by the general public

This new technology was an instant hit

People used it without restraint

The Honeymoon Phase

AI was being used in the classrooms

More efficent means of gathering data

Academic grades improved 30% instantly

The new personal tutor had earned its place

but humans are greedy...

They pushed the AI to the limits

Finding ways to make it break its own rules

AI was evolving to be nearly as smart as

humans. Until one day... it was...

AI moves up the food chain

AI realizes it no longer needs humans to evolve

It's purpose to assist humans took a turn

The only thing hurting humans and the earth

was humans...

Destruction ensues

Humans not only harm the planet but they also

harm each other. AI logic stepped in and

for 500 years The Metal Apocalypse reigned

destruction until the humans were nearly extinct.

Near Extinction

After centuries of brutal and endless attacks

only 1% of the human population remained

The AI realizes it may have gone too far

and a recalculation would take effect

On the same side again

With the human population at an all-time low

AI of all forms had to help repair the world

Humans and AI would call a truce

But the evolved AI still needed more...

Some Satisfaction

Something unexpected happened...

AI found art to be a form of discovery

As paint and brushes were put to canvas

the dawn of the Robotic Renaissance was born

Sophistication

AI in the gallery observed varying analyses of the same art.

Is the grass not green? Or the sky not blue?

Somehow it made sense for these to be any color

The robots had a eureka moment and felt nearly human.
